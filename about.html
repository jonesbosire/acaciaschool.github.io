Welcome To Acacia School

We Learn Smart Way To Build Bright Futute For Your Children

At Acacia School, we cultivate the young minds and hearts of children aged 2-6 years in a safe and nurturing environment. Guided by our values, we go beyond traditional classrooms, offering a Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) with value addition. This unique approach ensures your child thrives not just academically, but also develops strong leadership, talent, and character skills.

From playful exploration to creative expression, our programs spark curiosity, critical thinking, and social-emotional development.
