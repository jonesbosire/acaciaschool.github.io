Acacia School Crech'es offer secure and stimulating environments designed to cultivate the unique needs of both toddlers (Crech'e 1) and preschoolers (Crech'e 2). Through engaging activities, playful exploration, and expert guidance, we lay the foundation for a lifetime of learning and joy.

Crech'e 1

Designed for ages 2-3, Creche 1 provides a secure and stimulating space where curiosity and discovery flourish. Our dedicated team of caregivers understands the unique needs of toddlers, ensuring that every child receives personalized attention and care. Through engaging activities, sensory exploration, and gentle guidance, we lay the foundation for cognitive, emotional, and social development.

Crech'e 2

Creche 2 targets approximately ages 3-4. Our experienced educators craft age-appropriate experiences that encourage cognitive, social, and readiness to read and write skills development. Through hands-on activities, storytelling, and interactive play, we nurture young minds and inspire them to ask questions, make connections, and embark on their educational journey with enthusiasm.

  • Class Experience

    Crech'e 1 (2-3): Tiny explorers delve into sensory play, move and groove with music, and learn alongside loving caregivers, building social skills and routines.

    Crech'e 2 (3-4): Hands-on learning through painting, building, and playtime takes center stage, while stories, songs, and collaborative play introduce pre-reading, writing, and problem-solving skills.

  • Learning Goals

    At Acacia School, we know play is powerful! From the youngest toddlers to pre-schoolers, our Crech'es are buzzing with fun activities that help your child grow in amazing ways. Through sensory play, music, and playtime with friends, they build important social and emotional skills, learning to share, be kind, and make new friends.

  • Teacher Expertise

    All Crech'e teachers are qualified early childhood professionals with experience and training in child development. They are loving, nurturing, and patient individuals who understand the unique needs of young children and are passionate about creating a safe and stimulating learning environment where children can thrive.

  • Time Commitment

    Crech'e 1 and 2 offer flexible scheduling options to meet your family's needs. We offer full-day, half-day, and drop-in programs. We encourage you to visit our school and discuss your specific needs with our admissions team.
