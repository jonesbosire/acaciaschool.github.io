Welcome To Acacia

At Acacia School, we believe in nurturing young minds and hearts

We believe in nurturing future leaders and change-makers by planting the seeds of excellence in children aged 2-6 years. Guided by Christian values and a mission to empower young minds, we offer a holistic learning experience that goes beyond traditional classrooms.

At Acacia School, your child feels right at home! We have comfy classrooms for exploring and learning, and teachers who give special attention to help them grow their brains, hearts, and leadership skills. We're like a big family where everyone helps each other love learning. Choose Acacia, and watch your child blossom!
